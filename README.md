**Polkadot Space Fighting** is a space battle between a **Werewolf** and a **Killer Robot**. The game's style combines the vastness of space with the unique visual textures of Polkadot and a strong dose of creativity.

The Werewolves in the game are inspired by **speculative evolutionary theories**, while the Killer Robots are the persona of **Polkadot's Parachains**.
